Pi Day Mathematics Conference

Celebrate the wonders of mathematics with us this March 14th at the Grand Mathema Hotel. This year's theme, "Circles of Infinity," explores the endless possibilities within the realm of circular geometry. Keynote speakers include the renowned Dr. Helena Circle and Prof. Radius Diameter. Don't miss the pi recitation contest and pie tasting event!

Math in Nature Exhibit Opening

Opening this April 22nd, our newest exhibit delves into the fascinating world where mathematics meets the natural world. From the spirals of sunflowers to the fractal patterns of snowflakes, discover the hidden mathematical rules that govern our environment. Interactive installations and guided tours available daily.

Quantum Mathematics Workshop

Scheduled for September 5th-9th at the Quantum Computing Center, this intensive workshop invites math enthusiasts to explore the cutting-edge field of quantum mathematics. Led by quantum theorist Dr. Qubit Entangle and mathematician Prof. Alice Bobson, participants will engage in hands-on sessions on quantum algorithms, entanglement theory, and the math behind quantum computing. Space is limited, register early!
